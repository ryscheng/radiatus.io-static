Our Manifesto

You deserve the right to control your data and experience on the Web.

Radiatus.io is a radically different vision of the future of the Web, where web apps run on infrastructure owned by the users, not the developers. When you access a Radiatus web app and store private data, all interactions occur between you and your own web server. When you share thoughts or moments with your friends and your family, those communications occur directly between your web server and theirs. If advertisers or government bodies want access to your data, they can either request consent or get a subpeona.

When we use web applications today, the majority of computation and storage over your data happens on servers owned and operated by the developer of the website and their partners. In essence for consumers, today the cloud is synonymous with a giant computer you don't own or have any control over. You are trusting that the website, the associated company, its potentially many employees, and their partners protect your interests.

While many websites do a fine job, it is no news that your interests are not always prioritized. Free websites are incentivized to sell your data to marketers. Some companies don't have the budget to secure their website and protect you from attacks and data leakages are becoming commonplace. Governments have set up agreements with companies to obtain data without due process.

It doesn't have to be this way.

Some people trivialize these issues as the death of privacy, but we don't see it that way. We have a right to determine how our data is used and shared. We have a right to determine who can see the data that we generate. We have a right over the course of our digital lives, which is becoming more important over time.

The right to life has come to mean the right to enjoy life, the right to be let alone; the right to liberty secures the exercise of extensive civil privileges; and the term "property" has grown to comprise every form of possession -- intangible, as well as tangible.

"The Right to Privacy", Samuel D. Warren and Louis D. Brandeis

What We'll Offer

For Users

Control Your Web Apps

Since it's your server, we'll give you power over it. Customize your web apps with plugins. Add support for your favorite social network or database, even before your web app developer does.

Move Your Server

It's yours. Move it whereever you want. You can host your server from a variety of cloud providers or even your own desktop.

No More Lock-in

Don't let websites tell you what you can and cannot do with your data. The Radiatus web server is and will always be open source. That means you'll be able to extract your data at any time as well as run the web app yourself.

Pay For What You Use

Don't pay for a web app with your data and soul. We'll give all users a server with limited hardware and storage to start for free. We'll only charge you if you want more.

For Developers

Free Scaling

Most PaaS cloud providers give you a free tier, but you'll feel the pinch when you get big. On Radiatus.io, we'll never charge you for hosting, that's the user's job. Go ahead, grow to a billion users.

No More Operations

Stick to the fun stuff, software development. You'll no longer need to stress about keeping your site up, we'll do that for you. That means if you're an open source developer, you can instantly deploy for free!

Make Money

Sell your application on our app store, or sell virtual goods with in-app purchases. Instant deployment at scale makes it trivial for newcomers to challenge goliaths in the industry.

Feel Good

Knowing that you're decentralizing the Web and giving users real rights.
