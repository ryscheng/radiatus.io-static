Our Manifesto

You deserve the right to control your data and experience on the Web.

Radiatus.io is a radically different vision of the future of the Web, where web apps run on infrastructure owned by the users, not the developers. When you access a Radiatus web app and store private data, all interactions occur between you and a web server that you own. When you share thoughts or moments with your friends and your family, those communications occur directly between your web server and theirs. If advertisers or government bodies want access to your data, they can either request consent or get a subpeona.

Web apps today have incredible power

When we use modern web applications, the majority of computation and storage of our data happens on servers we don't have control over. For consumers, the cloud is synonymous with a giant computer you can't touch. You are trusting that the website, the data center, the potentially many employees, and affiliated companies will all protect your interests.

That power is occasionally abused

While many websites do a fine job, it is no news that your interests are not always prioritized. Free websites are incentivized to sell your data to marketers. Small companies don't have the budget to secure their website and protect your data from attacks, making data leakages commonplace. Some governments have agreements with websites to obtain data without due process.

Let's empower the Web generation

Some people defeatedly portray these issues as the death of privacy, but we don't see it like that.

  • We have the right to own and control any thought we express or action we take online.
  • We have the right to determine who can see the data that we generate.
  • We have the right to use our data as we like, without being subject to lock-in.
  • We have the right to control the course of our digital lives.

The right to life has come to mean the right to enjoy life, the right to be let alone; the right to liberty secures the exercise of extensive civil privileges; and the term "property" has grown to comprise every form of possession -- intangible, as well as tangible.

"The Right to Privacy", Samuel D. Warren and Louis D. Brandeis

The people want their data back

Any CEO of an advertising company is fooling you if they say otherwise. We're seeing a surge of startups now offering private clouds for businesses. Encryption is becoming more commonplace in everything from messaging apps to file storage. Users resort to tools that violate your privacy, not because users don't value their privacy, but because there aren't enough alternatives. At the end of the day, it is hard to write a web app that preserves your privacy.

The current model for web apps needs to evolve

Existing web apps have pushed the boundaries of what we can do on the Web and how we communicate and operate as a society, but existing web frameworks don't give users the privacy they deserve.

Let's make extreme auto-federation of web apps trivial

Radiatus is a web framework that aims to solve the common challenges in making a web app federated. Our goal is to make it so easy that we can take federation to the extreme, where every single user has their own web server. Because Radiatus web apps are easily federated, users can migrate their servers off of ours onto any cloud provider or even their own desktop. We do usability right, just because your data is yours doesn't mean apps have to be any more than a browser bookmark away. What types of opportunities can we open up with more control over our online data?

What We'll Offer

For Users

Control Your Web Apps

Since it's your server, we'll give you power over it. Customize your Radiatus web apps with plugins. Add support for your favorite social network or database, even before the developer does.

Move Your Server

It's yours. Move it whereever you want. You can host your server from a variety of cloud providers or even your own desktop.

No More Lock-in

Don't let websites tell you what you can and cannot do with your data. The Radiatus web server is and will always be open source. That means you'll be able to extract your data at any time as well as run any Radiatus web app yourself.

Pay For What You Use

Don't pay for a web app with your data and soul. We'll give all users a server with limited hardware and storage to start for free. We'll only charge you if you want more.

For Developers

Free Scaling

Most PaaS cloud providers give you a free tier, but you'll feel the pinch when you get big. On Radiatus.io, we'll never charge you for hosting, that's the user's job. Go ahead, grow to a billion users.

No More Operations

Stick to the fun stuff, software development. You'll no longer need to stress about keeping your site up, we'll do that for you. That means if you're an open source developer, you can instantly deploy for free!

Make Money

Take donations, show advertisements, sell software and licenses on our app store, or charge for real world services. There are plenty of business models that don't betray user trust.

Feel Good

Knowing that you're decentralizing the Web and giving users real rights.
